My Favorite Superheroes

Deadpool

This superhero is a badass with no filter. He went through a painful procedure to get rid of cancer, which in turn gave him the power to regenerate and not feel pain.

Michelangelo

This is a member of the Ninja Turles squad. He is a beast with nunchuks. Also, as you can see, he devours pizza.

Iron Man

Super rich guy who built a body suit of armor. Kind of a prick, but he saves people. I guess he's chill.
